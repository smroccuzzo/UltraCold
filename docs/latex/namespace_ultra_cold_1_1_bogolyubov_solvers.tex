\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
This namespace contains solver classes for several flavours of Bogolyubov equations. 

While the GPSolver class and related classes allows to study the ground state and the dynamics of Bose-\/\+Einstein condensates, the elementary excitations on top of a certain state can be studied by solving the so-\/called {\itshape Bogolyubov equations}. The idea is to consider a certain configuration, described a condensate wave-\/function $ \psi_0 $, and with chemical potential $ \mu $, and to study small oscillations on top of it by searching for solutions of the Gross-\/\+Pitaevskii equation of the form

\[
 \psi({\bf r},t) = e^{-i\frac{\mu}{\hbar}t}\left[ \psi_0({\bf r})
 + \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \left( u_n({\bf r})e^{-i\omega_n t} + v^*_n({\bf r})e^{i\omega_n t} \right) \right]
\]

Keeping only terms linear the functions $ u $ and $ v $, one finds that the {\itshape quasi-\/particle amplitudes} $ u_n $ and $ v_n $ and their energies $ \hbar\omega_n $ are given by the solutions of a certain eigenvalue problem. The form depends on the \char`\"{}flavour\char`\"{} of Gross-\/\+Pitaevskii equation one is considering (ordinary, dipolar, mixtures...) and is described in the documentation of the specific solver classes.
